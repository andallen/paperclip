Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 26, 2026

PaperClip is a note-taking app for iPad. Your privacy is simple: the app does not collect any data.

What PaperClip Does Not Do

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not require an account or sign-in
  • Does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising
  • Does not send data to any server
  • Does not include third-party SDKs that collect data

Local Storage

Your drawings are saved as files in the app's private, sandboxed storage on your device. They are not uploaded anywhere and are not accessible to other apps.

Clipboard

When you tap the Send button, PaperClip copies your drawing to the system clipboard. This uses Apple's standard clipboard API. The app does not read from or monitor the clipboard at any other time.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
