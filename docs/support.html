Getting Started

  1. Open PaperClip on your iPad
  2. Write or draw with Apple Pencil
  3. Tap the Send button to copy your drawing to the clipboard
  4. The drawing is available on any device signed into the same iCloud account via Universal Clipboard

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my drawing onto my Mac?

PaperClip uses Apple's Universal Clipboard. When you tap Send, the drawing is copied to your clipboard and automatically syncs to your Mac if both devices are on the same iCloud account with Handoff enabled.

Where are my notes stored?

Notes are saved locally on your iPad in the app's private storage. They are not uploaded to any server.

Can I use my finger to draw?

Drawing is Apple Pencil only. Use your finger to scroll and navigate the sidebar.
