Getting Started

  1. Install PaperClipReceiver on your Mac (the companion receiver app)
  2. Open PaperClip on your iPad
  3. Write or draw with Apple Pencil
  4. Tap the Send button — your drawing is sent directly to your Mac over peer-to-peer Wi-Fi
  5. Paste (⌘V) into any app on your Mac

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my drawing onto my Mac?

PaperClip sends drawings directly to your Mac over peer-to-peer Wi-Fi using Apple Wireless Direct Link (AWDL). A small companion app, PaperClipReceiver, runs on your Mac and places the received drawing on your clipboard so you can paste it anywhere.

Where are my notes stored?

Notes are saved locally on your iPad in the app's private storage. They are not uploaded to any server.

Can I use my finger to draw?

Drawing is Apple Pencil only. Use your finger to scroll and navigate the sidebar.
