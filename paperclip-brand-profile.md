# PaperClip — Mockingbird Brand Profile

## App
- **Name:** PaperClip
- **Platform:** iPad-only
- **OS requirement:** iOS 23+

## Devices
- iPad-13 (2064 × 2752)
- iPad-12.9 (2048 × 2732)
- iPad-11 (1668 × 2388)

## Screenshot Settings
- **Screen content:** Transparent
- **Languages:** English only
- **Screenshot source:** Explore codebase and generate (option B)

## Brand Colors
| Role | Hex |
|------|-----|
| Background (paper) | `#F7F7F5` |
| Primary text (ink) | `#1F1C1A` |
| Secondary text | `#595552` |
| Faint / disabled | `#8C8A87` |

## Typography
- **Font:** SF Pro Display — `-apple-system, 'SF Pro Display', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif`
- **Weight:** Bold (700)
- **Letter spacing:** `-0.02em`
- **Rule:** Sans-serif only. No serifs. Clean and techy.

## Style Direction
- **Mood:** Editorial
- **Description:** Minimal and clean — lots of whitespace, devices floating on paper background like a magazine spread. No gradients, no shadows, no depth effects. Flat and confident.
- **Background:** Solid paper-white (`#F7F7F5`) — matches the app's own canvas color.

## Composition Notes
- Devices should have **transparent screens** (background color shows through)
- For panoramic two-slot images: headline on left slot, second line on right slot
- Apple Pencil is part of the iPad visual — angled alongside the device
- MacBook and iPad pair naturally for the "handwriting → AI" story
